Why Your Contribution Matters

Recycling is one of the most effective ways to protect our environment. It conserves natural resources, reduces pollution, saves energy, and decreases the amount of waste sent to landfills.

By recycling a single plastic bottle, you can save enough energy to power a light bulb for 3 hours. Every item you recycle with IRECYCLE contributes to a healthier planet for future generations.

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the service completely free?

Using the IRECYCLE app is completely free, and you can easily request a pickup for your recyclables. However, a small delivery fee is charged for the pickup service. In return, you earn points for every item you recycle!

What areas do you cover?

We are constantly expanding! You can check the currently available areas directly within the app by entering your address.

How are points calculated?

Points are awarded based on the type and quantity of materials collected. Each material has a specific point value per number of items. For example, 5 plastic bottles give you 20 points, 5 glass bottles give 40 points, and 5 aluminum cans give 50 points. You can check the full point system in the app. Every 20 points equals 1 EGP in rewards.
